About

Founded to support the growing need for quality competition, Braz Amer Jiu Jitsu is dedicated to providing a unique platform that recognizes and rewards athletes unlike any other competition. Our aim is to create an inclusive community where both competitors and practitioners can thrive, fostering a spirit of excellence and camaraderie. At Braz Amer Jiu Jitsu, we're not just about competition; we're about serving. As a veteran-owned company, we're deeply committed to growing Jiu Jitsu through service. Join us for an unforgettable grappling experience that not only showcases the best in competition but also celebrates the rich connections made through martial arts. Medals are awarded to top placers, and our tournaments emphasize both gi and no-gi divisions, as well as absolute divisions. Our simplified rule set ensures transparency and fairness, with no advantages awarded. We welcome participants of all levels, from experienced athletes to casual practitioners, to be a part of our community.



Simplified Rule Set Highlights

  • No Advantages Awarded: In our competitions, no advantages are awarded, ensuring transparency and clarity in determining victory.
  • Gi and No-Gi Divisions: Our tournaments feature both gi and no-gi divisions to accommodate different preferences and styles.
  • Absolute Divisions: We offer absolute divisions for those seeking additional challenges and opportunities for recognition.
